A little about me

I'm an entrepreneur, a tech head and an aspiring Data Scientist
-
I love having control (I mean who doesn't). This website for example - I control the design and the architecture. For my projects, I try to streamline the process to make the different teams work in tandem.
This is what draws me towards data. Working with big data provides the kind of control I like. The feeling of being overwhelmed by millions of datapoints and making sense of it. Manipulating it and visualising it to make algorithms - It makes me feel alive!

Highlights

Findr

An mobile app that helps students find study partners for their courses, projects and hackathons Being deployed to 5 universities across North America and is expected to have a user base of over 10,000 students.

Fixit

A recommendation engine (endorsed by the Univeristy of Toronto) that recommends personalised review questions to students based on their past answers Built using Collaborative Filtering and Auto Encoder and deployed on Django and integrated with the university servers. Being used by professors and students at UofT

Spotify

Personal project where I use the data collected form Spotify API to perform Time Series Analysis and build a content based recommendation system Deployed using Flask and Heroku.
